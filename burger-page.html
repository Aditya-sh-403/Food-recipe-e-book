Juicy, flame-grilled beef patty topped with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, tangy pickles, and melted cheddar, all nestled in a freshly toasted bun. Every bite is a flavorful, satisfying delight.

  • Ingredients:
    For the Patty:
    2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, and beans), finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs (optional, for binding)
  • 1/4 cup grated paneer (optional, for extra richness)
    For Assembly:
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Mint chutney or tamarind chutney (optional, for spreading)
  • Ketchup or mayonnaise (optional)
    Instructions:
  • Prepare the Patties:
    Boil the potatoes in a pot of water until tender. Drain and mash them in a large bowl. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds, and let them splutter. Add finely chopped mixed vegetables to the pan and saut√© for 5 minutes until they are cooked. Add turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well. Combine the cooked vegetables with the mashed potatoes. Add fresh coriander and mix thoroughly. If the mixture is too soft, add bread crumbs for binding and grated paneer for richness. Shape into 4 patties.
  • Cook the Patties:
    Heat a little oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook each patty for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  • Prepare the Buns:
    Slice the burger buns in half. Toast the buns lightly on a pan or in a toaster until golden brown, if desired.
  • Assemble the Burger:
    Spread mint chutney or tamarind chutney on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf, a patty, a slice of tomato, cucumber, and onion on top. Add ketchup or mayonnaise if you like. Cover with the top half of the bun.
  • Serve:
    Serve the burgers immediately while hot. Enjoy your flavorful, homemade Indian-style veg burgers!

Serve your Indian-style veg burgers hot, bursting with vibrant spices and fresh vegetables. Perfect for a hearty meal, they offer a delicious fusion of flavors that will leave everyone craving more.
