Cooking noodles is a great skill for any beginner. Here’s a simple and delicious recipe for making basic noodles with a savory sauce:

    Ingredients:
  • 8 ounces (about 225 grams) of dried noodles (spaghetti, egg noodles, or any type you prefer)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 cup (240 ml) of low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional for heat)
  • 2 green onions, sliced (optional for garnish)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional for garnish)
    Equipment:
  • A large pot
  • A colande
  • A large skillet or wok
  • A small bowl
  • A spoon or whisk
    Instructions:
  • Cook the Noodles:
    Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil over high heat. Add a pinch of salt if you like.
    Once boiling, add the dried noodles and cook according to the package instructions (usually around 8-10 minutes) until they are tender but still firm. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
    When cooked, drain the noodles in a colander and rinse them briefly with cold water to stop cooking. Set aside.
  • Prepare the Sauce:
    In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sugar, rice vinegar, and red pepper flakes (if using). Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. Set aside.
  • Cook the Aromatics:
    Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
    If using garlic, add it to the skillet and cook for about 1 minute, stirring constantly, until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  • Combine Everything:
    Add the cooked noodles to the skillet with the garlic. Stir to coat the noodles with the oil and garlic.
    Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles. Toss everything together to ensure the noodles are evenly coated and heated through. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  • Garnish and Serve:
    Transfer the noodles to a serving dish. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired

And that’s it! You’ve made a quick and tasty noodle dish. Enjoy it as a main course or as a side with your favorite proteins and vegetables.
